## Best Practices

<Note>
For effective daily reviews:
- Set a consistent review schedule
- Tag important insights for future reference
- Create action items from relevant discoveries
- Transfer valuable insights to permanent Notes
</Note>

## Building a Review Habit

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="clock" title="Timing Your Review">
    - Schedule reviews at consistent times
    - Allocate sufficient time for reflection
    - Combine with your existing daily routines
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="star" title="Making It Meaningful">
    - Focus on quality over quantity
    - Look for actionable insights
    - Track progress on recurring themes
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
Remember to:
- Be consistent with your review practice
- Don't let reviews accumulate unprocessed
- Take action on important insights promptly
</Warning>

## Long-term Benefits

The daily review practice in Blinko serves as your personal knowledge curator, helping you:
- Transform fleeting thoughts into lasting insights
- Develop a more structured thinking process
- Build a valuable repository of ideas and inspirations
- Create connections between seemingly unrelated concepts

By maintaining a consistent review practice, you'll develop a more organized and intentional approach to personal knowledge management, ensuring that no valuable insight goes unnoticed or unused.
