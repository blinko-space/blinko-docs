## Overview

Blinko comes with an elegant global music player that transforms your note-taking environment into a more immersive experience. Whether you're brainstorming, writing, or reviewing notes, having the right music can boost your creativity and productivity.

## Why Music in Blinko?

Research shows that music can significantly impact our cognitive performance:

- **Enhanced Focus**: Background music can help maintain concentration during note-taking
- **Improved Creativity**: The right music can stimulate creative thinking
- **Better Memory**: Musical accompaniment can aid in information retention
- **Mood Management**: Create the perfect atmosphere for different types of work

## Getting Started

1. Configure your Spotify API credentials in settings:
  <img className="rounded-2xl" src="/images/2024-12-23-18-19-00.png" />

2. Upload your MP3 files using the upload button in the bottom toolbar

3. Blinko will automatically:
   - Process your audio files
   - Fetch metadata and cover art
   - Create a beautiful player interface

## Usage Tips

- Create themed playlists for different types of notes
- Use tags like `#music` to organize your audio content
- Add music files to specific notes for context-relevant soundtracks

<Note>
The music player is designed to be non-intrusive while providing quick access to your audio library. The player stays minimized at the top of your screen until you need it.
</Note>

## Technical Details

The music player leverages Spotify's powerful API to:
- Identify songs and artists
- Fetch high-quality album artwork
- Provide accurate metadata
- Ensure proper audio organization

This integration ensures your music collection stays well-organized and visually appealing within Blinko.
